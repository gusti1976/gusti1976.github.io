About the Song

"My Fire (Mi Fuego)" is a passionate bilingual love song that seamlessly blends English and Spanish to create an intimate anthem about queer love, devotion, and togetherness. The song's central message - "Nunca solos / Never alone" - speaks to the profound connection between lovers who face the world united.

Written and produced by Agust Smari Bjarkarson (performing as Agust Islandia), "My Fire" uses the metaphor of fire (fuego) to represent passion, warmth, and the shared energy between two people in love. The bilingual structure creates a beautiful call-and-response dynamic: "You say nunca solos / I say never alone" - two voices, two languages, one love.

The song opens with a dedication: "Esto es para los amantes, para los sue√±os" (This is for the lovers, for the dreams) - positioning queer love as something worth celebrating, worth fighting for. Lines like "Para todos los que avanzan sin miedo" (For all those who move forward without fear) speak to the courage required to love openly and authentically.

Perfect for LGBTQ+ weddings, Pride celebrations, or any moment celebrating queer romance, "My Fire (Mi Fuego)" is both intimate and universal - a love song that speaks to anyone who has found their person and promises: "I'll make sure you're never alone."

Bilingual Love: Why Code-Switching Matters

The song's use of code-switching (alternating between Spanish and English) is not just stylistic - it's deeply meaningful for LGBTQ+ representation:

  • Cultural Bridge: Connects English and Spanish-speaking queer communities
  • Dual Identity: Reflects the experience of bilingual/bicultural LGBTQ+ individuals
  • Call and Response: "You say nunca solos / I say never alone" - Two voices in harmony
  • Inclusivity: Makes LGBTQ+ love songs accessible to Latinx community
  • Authenticity: Natural language mixing reflects how many bilingual people actually communicate
