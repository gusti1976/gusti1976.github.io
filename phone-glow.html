About the Song

"Phone Glow" deepens the betrayal introduced in "Two of Us." The protagonist is now fully immersed in late-night app scrolling, lying awake next to his sleeping partner, consumed by an endless stream of profiles and messages.

The imagery of the "phone glow" becomes a metaphor for the light of deception‚Äîilluminating the protagonist's face in the dark while hiding the truth from his partner. The song captures the addictive, compulsive nature of hookup apps and the self-deception that enables betrayal: "Tomorrow I'll be good today."
