About the Song

"First Session" captures the terrifying yet necessary moment of walking into therapy for the first time. The protagonist wrestles with doubt — "do I really need therapy?" — while the physical details of an oversized chair and too-wide room convey vulnerability and smallness in the face of healing.

The song's repeated question, "If I speak aloud will I breathe again?", drives home the musical's core belief: that breaking silence is the first act of survival. By the final lines, doubt gives way to the fragile, hopeful possibility of belonging.
