October 2025 - The Unseen Chorus Musical Released

Release Date: October 6, 2025

Agust Islandia released The Unseen Chorus, A Gay Musical, a 27-track theatrical work documenting a journey from infidelity through sexual addiction to recovery and redemptive love. The full site now supports the release with official lyrics and track-level pages.

The project addresses hookup culture, anonymous sex, betrayal, divorce, shame, and recovery through explicitly queer storytelling rather than neutralized abstract pop writing.
