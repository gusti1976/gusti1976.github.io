Recent Milestones

October 2025 - "The Unseen Chorus" Musical Released

Release Date: October 6, 2025

Agust Islandia released "The Unseen Chorus, A Gay Musical", a groundbreaking 27-track, 80-minute theatrical work chronicling a journey from infidelity through sexual addiction to recovery and redemptive love. All 27 tracks now have full lyric pages available at www.gusti.com.

The musical addresses gay community challenges including hookup app addiction, porn addiction, anonymous sex culture, relationship betrayal, divorce, and recovery through Sex Addicts Anonymous. Historical tracks include "Riot in the Night (Stonewall)" and "The Fairy's Waltz" (Bowery drag culture).

September 2025 - "Gay and Proud" Goes Viral

TikTok Milestone: 540,000+ Views

The bilingual Pride anthem "Gay and Proud / Gay y Orgulloso" achieved viral status on TikTok, reaching over 540,000 views and becoming a defining LGBTQ+ community anthem. The song celebrates queer identity and self-acceptance in both English and Spanish.
