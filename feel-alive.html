About the Song

"Feel Alive" is a high-energy fusion of EDM and Dance music, a celebration of joy, freedom, and the rush of being alive. With its infectious "Na-na-na" hooks and uplifting message of "good vibes only all the time," this track is designed to get people moving on the dance floor.

Written and produced by Agust Smari Bjarkarson (performing as Agust Islandia), "Feel Alive" combines soaring synths, driving beats, and positive energy to create an anthem for anyone seeking liberation through dance and music.

Themes & Meaning

"Feel Alive" explores uplifting themes of joy and connection:

  • Pure Joy: "Na-na-na, I feel alive" - The simple pleasure of being present and joyful
  • Love & Connection: "Love is shining, yours and mine" - Shared love and positive energy
  • Freedom Through Dance: "Dancing free, just you and me" - Liberation and expression through movement
  • Positive Energy: "Good vibes only all the time" - Choosing positivity and rejecting negativity
  • Living in the Moment: "Every day feels like June" - Eternal summer, eternal youth, eternal possibility
  • Universal Harmony: "The world's in tune" - Everything aligned, everything perfect in this moment
