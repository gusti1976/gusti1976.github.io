Why This Song Matters

"Just an Advisory" opens The Unseen Chorus with a compassionate content warning. This spoken-word introduction acknowledges the difficult themes explored in the musical—addiction, infidelity, mental health struggles—while offering immediate support resources.

By referencing SA.org (Sex Addicts Anonymous), the song sets a tone of honesty and care, inviting the audience to step into challenging subject matter with support available. It's an invitation: "Together."
