About the Song

"Gay and Proud" is a thunderous LGBTQ+ pride anthem that declares "No more whispers, I stand in the crowd." Released in 2025, this empowering single became a viral sensation on TikTok with over 540,000 views, establishing itself as a defining gay pride anthem for the LGBTQ+ community worldwide.

The song celebrates coming out loud and proud with soaring choruses, rainbow imagery, and the rallying cry "Love is love and it won't be tamed." Through infectious dance energy and bold storytelling, "Gay and Proud" has become an anthem for queer visibility, community strength, and unapologetic self-acceptance.

Written and produced by Agust Smari Bjarkarson (performing as Agust Islandia), the track combines powerful lyrics about gay pride with an infectious dance beat, making it perfect for Pride Month celebrations and LGBTQ+ events.

Themes & Meaning

"Gay and Proud" explores powerful themes central to the LGBTQ+ experience:

  • Coming Out: "No more whispers, I stand in the crowd" - The journey from hiding to visibility
  • Self-Acceptance: Embracing queer identity with pride and confidence
  • Community Strength: "We are one" - The power of LGBTQ+ solidarity
  • Love Without Limits: "Love is love and it won't be tamed" - Universal love and equality
  • Pride Celebration: Rainbow imagery and unapologetic joy
  • Defiance & Liberation: "Break every chain" - Freedom from societal constraints
