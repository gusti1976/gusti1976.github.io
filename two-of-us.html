About the Song

"Two of Us" is the devastating opening number of The Unseen Chorus's narrative arc. What begins as a tender portrait of domestic love—coffee, socks, shared laughter—quickly reveals the cracks beneath the surface. The protagonist lies next to his partner while secretly engaging with hookup apps in the dark.

The song captures the heartbreaking contradiction at the core of infidelity: genuine love coexisting with compulsive betrayal. "Love's not enough when the hunger's alive" articulates the beginning of a spiral that will define the entire musical.
