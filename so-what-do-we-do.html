Why This Song Matters

So, What Do We Do? is the first serious decision-point in the relationship. It pivots from chemistry toward logistics, commitment, and the reality of shared direction.

What makes the song so powerful is its honesty — neither character knows the "rules," and in that uncertainty they discover something more valuable: someone who sees them completely and still wants more. The closing lines are among the most celebrated in the musical.
