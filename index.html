About

Agust Smari Bjarkarson, performing as Agust Islandia, is an Icelandic musician, songwriter, and producer from Reykjavik. Born on August 26, 1976, his musical journey began with a childhood discovery that would shape his artistic path: "One of my earliest memories is learning to use my parents' vinyl record player. I was hooked on the crackle of the needle and the endless albums, feeling like each song revealed a secret."

Forged in the land of fire and ice and seasoned by a life in motion, Agust Islandia creates music at the edge of extremes. His sound is an alchemy of volcanic silence and electric energy, spanning shimmering synth-pop anthems, cinematic ballads, and atmospheric electronica. He masterfully blends deep soundscapes with high-energy beats and melodic storytelling, creating music that resonates both locally and internationally through DistroKid's worldwide distribution.
