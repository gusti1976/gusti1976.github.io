Agust Smari Bjarkarson, Agust Islandia

Agust Smari Bjarkarson, known artistically as Agust Islandia, is an Icelandic pop artist from Reykjavik. His music blends humor, heart, and bold storytelling with dance energy, celebrating identity and freedom. With releases spanning Pride anthems to theatrical projects, he inspires creativity and connection.
