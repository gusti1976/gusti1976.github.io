About Agust Islandia

Agust Islandia is the artist project of Agust Smari Bjarkarson, an Icelandic songwriter, producer, and musical storyteller focused on queer pop and LGBTQ+ musical theatre. His catalog is built around emotionally direct lyric writing with theatrical structure, so songs work both as individual tracks and as chapters inside larger narrative arcs.

Two flagship works define this era: Swipe Me to the Moon (A Gay Musical) and The Unseen Chorus (A Gay Musical). Together, they document contemporary queer life from first digital connection to intimacy, conflict, grief, accountability, and chosen love. The method is story-first: recurring themes, character growth, and lyrical continuity across full albums.

Outside the musicals, singles such as Gay and Proud, My Fire (Mi Fuego), and Feel Alive expand the project into bilingual anthems, dance-forward releases, and public declarations of queer visibility. This website exists as an official lyrics and discography archive so listeners, press, and researchers can move from artist biography to release pages to complete lyrics in one place.

Agust Islandia’s direction is clear: write songs with emotional truth, publish full lyrical context, and maintain a long-term queer music archive that stays accessible, human, and culturally useful globally.
