Agust Smari Bjarkarson (Agust Islandia)

Agust Smari Bjarkarson, known artistically as Agust Islandia, is an Icelandic pop artist from Reykjavik. His music blends humor, heart, and bold storytelling with dance energy, celebrating identity and freedom. With releases that span theatrical projects and Pride anthems, he inspires creativity and connection.
