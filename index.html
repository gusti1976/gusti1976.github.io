About

Agust Smari Bjarkarson, also known as Agust Islandia, is a pop artist from Reykjavik, Iceland. His music blends humor, heart, and bold storytelling with infectious dance energy. Drawing inspiration from Pride culture, theatre, and personal experience, his songs celebrate identity, creativity, and freedom.

Agust has released singles and musical projects that highlight both playful and emotional themes. His growing catalog has reached audiences on Spotify, Apple Music, TikTok, and YouTube, where he continues to connect with listeners worldwide.
