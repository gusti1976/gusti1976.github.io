About the Song

"The Awakening" opens Swipe Me to the Moon with a burst of joyful self-discovery. The protagonist finally admits what he has always known: he is gay, and he loves it. The song moves from hesitant inner monologue to full-throated celebration, capturing the electric relief of coming out to oneself.

The lyrics paint a vivid picture of queer joy — drag brunches, Berlin karaoke bars, crying at Pixar — before landing with a perfect comic twist: now that he knows who he is, he urgently needs a date. It sets up the entire musical's dating-app journey with humor and heart.
