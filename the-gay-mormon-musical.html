The Gay Mormon Musical

A 24-track album by Agust Islandia, released August 14, 2025.

Artist: Agust Islandia
Release Date: August 14, 2025
Tracks: 24

Story Arc

The Gay Mormon Musical is a spoken-word musical narrative that uses satire, intimacy, and personal conflict to examine faith, identity, secrecy, and desire in a high-control religious setting.
