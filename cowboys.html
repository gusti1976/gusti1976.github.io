Story Arc

Artist: Agust IslandiaRelease Date: September 2, 2025Tracks: 5

Cowboys EP blends camp, grief, desire, and pride through a queer country lens. The first two songs weaponize humor and sexual frankness against app-era loneliness, while the middle track turns inward toward loss and memory.

The final movement shifts into community and affirmation, from inclusive dance-floor identity in "Y'all Means All" to public romantic declaration in "Two Steers in Love." The EP frames queer rural life as fully human: funny, messy, erotic, wounded, and hopeful.
