Gay and Proud / Gay y Orgulloso Milestone

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE • March 3, 2026 • Reykjavik, Iceland

By: Agust Smari Bjarkarson • March 3, 2026

Agust Islandia's "Gay and Proud / Gay y Orgulloso" Reports 540,000 TikTok Views and 4 Million+ TikTok Song Plays in 3 Months

Reykjavik, Iceland — March 3, 2026 — Icelandic recording artist Agust Islandia (Agust Smari Bjarkarson) has reported new audience milestones for his bilingual Pride release, "Gay and Proud / Gay y Orgulloso," a track positioned in the gay anthem and LGBT anthem category.

According to artist-published figures, the campaign has reached 540,000 TikTok views and more than 4 million TikTok song plays over the last three months, reflecting sustained traction in short-form discovery and Pride-related music usage.

The release combines English and Spanish lyrics and is part of Agust Islandia's broader LGBTQ+ catalog, which includes singles, full lyric publishing, and long-form musical projects hosted at gusti.com.
